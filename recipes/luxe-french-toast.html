Luxe French Toast

Homepage

Description

Rise and shine with a plate if delicious, luxe french toast that turns any morning into a special occasion.
This elevated take on a breskfast classic features a bright, citrusy flavor paired with just the right touch of sweetness.
Topped with fresh fruit, it's not only indulgent but also a nutritious way to start your day.
Wheater you're treating yourself or sharing with loved ones, this french toast is the perfect balance of comfort and refreshment.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup unsweetend almond milk or coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon of coconut sugar or brown sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 tablespoon cinnamon
  • zest deom 1 orange
  • 6-8 slices of bread
  • pure maple syrup
  • Banana, Strawberry slices and Blueberries

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, alomond(or coconut) milk, coonut or brown sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon and orange zest until well combined.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the mixure, allowing it to soak for a few seconds on each side.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium head and add a small amount of butter. Once melted, place soaked bread onto the skillet
  4. Cook each side for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Serve warm, topped with fresh banana and strawberry slices, blueberries and a drizzle of maple syrup.
