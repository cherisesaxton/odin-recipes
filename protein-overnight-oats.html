Overnight Oats

Homepage

Description

Wake up to ready-made goodness with overnight oats. a simlple nutritious way to start your day without the morning rush.
Prepared the night before, these oats soak up flavor and creaminess while you sleep, so all you have to do is grab and go.
Its the perfect balance of convenience, flavor and energy to power your morning.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsweetend plain almond milk
  • 3/4 Tbsp of chia seeds
  • 2 Tbsp natural salted peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup gluten free rolled oats
  • Sliced banana and strawberry

Directions

  • To mason jar or small bowl with a lid, add almond milk, chia seeds, peanut butter and maple syrup.
    The peanut butter doesnt need to be completely mixed with the almound milk.
  • Add oats and stir a few more times. Then press down with a spoon to ensure all oats have been moistened and are immersed in almond mil.
  • Cover securely with a lid or seal and set in the refrigerator overnight (or for atleast 6 hours) to set.
  • The next day, open and enjoy as is a garnish with desired toppings.
